About

Born from the
gaps between us.

Human-Doing began with a simple observation: incredible communities existed across Kansas City, but they operated in silos. Events overlapped. Resources weren't shared. People who needed connection couldn't find their way in.

Founder Roma Valentine built this platform through her work as Director of Operations coordinating events for 75–300+ people and watched what happened when community infrastructure actually worked. People healed. Friendships formed. Purpose emerged.

Human-Doing is the infrastructure layer that every community deserves: a shared calendar, a vetted practitioner network, collaborative events, and the operational backbone to make it all sustainable.

Newbies especially welcome. We are seed starters for everyone's journey.

The Bigger Vision

Community connection
as healthcare.

Loneliness kills more people than smoking. Social Determinants of Health are the #1 predictor of outcomes. Yet insurance doesn't pay for community connection. Human-Doing is building the infrastructure to change that — a bridge between intentional communities, natural health practitioners, and the modern healthcare system.

2026
Build Credibility
Workplace wellness workshops. Proving ability to deliver results and track outcomes. Building healthcare relationships.
$80K–$120K target
2026–2027
Build the Database
50–100+ vetted practitioners catalogued. 20+ community organizations documented. Outcome tracking begins.
$150K–$250K target
2027–2028
First Healthcare Pilots
Pilot programs with 3 healthcare systems. Tracking hospital readmissions, ER visits, medication adherence.
$50K–$100K per pilot grant

Platform Launch
SaaS licensing to healthcare systems. Research publication. Conference presentations. 5–10 person team.
$300K–$2M target

Insurance Reimbursement
Lobbying for social prescribing CPT codes. CMS and major insurer partnerships. Every $1 in SDOH saves $3–7 in medical costs.
